This bookmarklet adds responsive functionalities to the Bonativo category page, right now intended for my mobile phone, an iPhone 6.

Drag and drop this bookmarklet into your bookmarks in your favorite web browser, I tested it in latest OSX Safari and Firefox:

Bonativo iPhone 6 Playground

Go to any category page in Bonativo, for example this one I used as a reference:

https://www.bonativo.de/de/berlin/obst-gemuese/

While browsing in that page, click on the bookmark you just added.
